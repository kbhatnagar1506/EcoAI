How EcoAI Works

Four powerful optimization strategies that make your AI greener without compromising quality

Prompt Compression

Intelligently removes filler words, optimizes structure, and enforces output constraints while preserving semantic meaning.

Workflow Pruning

Analyzes agent DAGs to identify and eliminate redundant steps, merge similar operations, and optimize execution paths.

Carbon-Aware Routing

Routes API calls to regions with lower carbon intensity and optimal timing while maintaining latency requirements.

Quality Gates

Multi-layer validation ensures optimization never compromises quality with automatic fallback to original prompts.
